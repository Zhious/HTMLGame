Introduction:

This is a game, here are some things to look forward to in the game:

  • Terrible Balance
  • Terrible Game Design
  • Terrible Art
  • Terrible UI
  • No saving/loading

Now that we have all the great things out of the way, we can get started.




Help:

  • Start with Mtn. Dungeon (lvl 1-5ish)
  • Every dungeon has a random number of enemies between 3-5, plus 1 boss at the end.
  • You gain xp and gold immediately after each kill
  • Remember to upgrade your gear in the store.
  • There is a Tier 5 sword and armor, but can only be obtained through drops from mobs.
  • You full heal upon leveling up, even mid-dungeon.
  • Most skills can be remapped while in town. Simply click the skill agin to remove it.
  • Dying makes you lose 50% of your gold. Escaping is only a 20% chance to lose half your gold, choose when to fight.
